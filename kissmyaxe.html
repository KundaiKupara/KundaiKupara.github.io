Kiss My Axe: Axe Throwing with a Twist

Kiss My Axe is a vibrant indoor entertainment venue in Sydney, Australia, offering a thrilling axe throwing experience in a safe, social, and professionally guided environment.

Locations

- Alexandria – Inner Sydney
- Brookvale – Northern Beaches
- Blacktown – Western Sydney

What Can You Do

- Throwing: Learn to throw axes with expert instructors in a controlled setting.
- Glow Darts & Glow Golf: Unique neon-lit games for added excitement.
- Archery & Pool Tables: More ways to test your aim and unwind.
- Mario Kart & Giant Jenga: Casual fun for all ages.

Food & Drinks

Licensed venues with curated food packages and drinks ideal for relaxing after your "axe-perience."

Perfect For

- Birthdays, bucks/hens parties, date nights
- Corporate events, team building, leadership workshops
- Family outings and holiday celebrations

Reputation

Kiss My Axe has over 5,000 five-star reviews known for friendly staff, great vibes, and unforgettable experiences.
